Lemon curd cheesecake

Description

In case you want to bake an amazing frozen cheesecake here is a recipe for you!

Make sure to make this some hours before you want to eat since it needs to freeze completley, it could also be left in the freezer overnight.

Ingredients

Bottom layer

  • 180 g (12 pcs) Digestive or "Mariekex"
  • 75 g melted butter

Lemon curd

  • 2 lemons
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 dl icing sugar
  • 75 g butter

    • Filling

    • 250 g cream cheese, natural flavor (like Philadelphia)
    • 1 dl brown sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla powder
    • 1 teaspoon grated lemon peel
    • 2 dl whipped cream

    Steps

    Make the bottom layer

    1. Grease the mold lightly or use baking paper.
    2. Mix the biscuits together with the melted butter.
    3. Press the batter into the bottom of a regular pie mold or a mold with detachable edge, and set to cool down.

    Make the lemon curd

    1. Wash the lemons and finely grate the outer peel. (Save a teaspoon of it to the filling). Squeeze 1 deciliter juice out of the lemons.
    2. Whisk together all the ingredients for the cream except the butter and simmer slowly with stirring until thickened.
    3. Melt the butter in the mixture and remove from the heat. Leave to cool.

    Make the filling and put together

    1. Whisk together the cream and sugar. It should be lightly whipped but not runny. Mix in the cheese, lemon peel and vanilla sugar and mix smoothly.
    2. Put some of the lemon curd in the mold and spread out over the biscuit layer. Leave to cool for a while.
    3. Put the cheese filling in the mold and refrigerate in the freezer until the cake solidifies completely.
    4. Spread the lemon curd over the finished cake and freeze until it solidifies.
    5. Take the cake out of the freezer a while before serving and allow it to melt a bit.
