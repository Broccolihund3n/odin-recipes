Swedish "Kladdkaka"

Description

This is a good recipe to make something quick and easy in under 1 hour. A sticky chocolate cake that is very popular in Sweden.

There's different versions of this cake but this is the basic recipe with some extra decorations if you want

Ingredients

  • 100 g butter
  • 3 dl sugar
  • 1/2 krm (swedsih measurment) or 1/2 ml salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla sugar
  • 2 1/2 tablesoons cacao
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 dl flour
  • 2 tablespoons icing sugar

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 150Â°C
  2. Melt the butter.
  3. Mix sugar, salt, vanilla sugar and cacao in the melted butter.
  4. Mix in the eggs.
  5. Mix in the flour.
  6. Pour everything in a round mould with loos bottom part. Heat 35 min in the oven. The cake should still be sticky inside
  7. Sprinkle some icing sugar on top. (Alternative decoration as in the picture: cut out a piece of the cake and decorate with whipped cream, strawberries, nuts and sprinkle some cacao)
